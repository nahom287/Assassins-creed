The legendary Mentor of the Italian Assassins, now on a quest to uncover Altaïr's secrets.

The legendary Syrian Assassin whose memories Ezio experiences through the keys.

Leader of the Constantinople Assassins and Ezio's ally in the city.

A Venetian book dealer who assists Ezio in his search for the keys.
